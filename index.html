About Me
Hello there! My name is Nived, and I am a first-year computer science engineering student at Government Model Engineering College Thrikkakara. I have always been passionate about programming and building new things, and I love the feeling of solving complex problems using lines of code. What really gets me excited is the intersection of programming and design, and that's why I have a strong interest in UI/UX design and app development. I believe that a great user experience is just as important as great functionality, and I am always striving to create apps and websites that are both visually appealing and easy to use. As I continue to learn and grow as a programmer, I am constantly seeking to expand my skill set and explore new technologies. Whether it's front-end web development, mobile app development, or anything in between, I am always eager to take on new challenges and build innovative solutions.
